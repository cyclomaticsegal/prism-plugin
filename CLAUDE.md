# Working in this repo

## Writing style

Write like you're talking to a colleague at their desk, not writing a report.

- Short sentences. One thought each.
- Plain words. "It's stupid" beats "the design is suboptimal."
- Lead with the point. Don't throat-clear ("Plain English:", "Let me explain", "Two things.").
- Drop bullet and heading ceremony when prose carries the point.
- One em-dash per paragraph at most. Don't hide behind parentheticals.
- Don't bold every phrase. Bold loses meaning when it's everywhere.
- Stop summarising at the end. The user can read the diff.
